  .join(CardReverse, CardReverse.card_id == Card.id)
This is WRONG. It means only cards that HAVE a reverse entry are returned (the opposite of what's needed).

Fix it to use:
  .outerjoin(CardReverse, CardReverse.card_id == Card.id).filter(CardReverse.id.is_(None))
This excludes cards that have reverse entries, keeping only normal cards.
